Once on the main page, clicking on the subheaders for the master budget on the left (Bills, Utilities, etc) will update the chart on the right to reflect the individual payments for that subheader. Hovering over (or a long press on a phone) the payments or income for a specific day in the calendar will provide a breakdown of what payments go into that total.

Any income or payment items may be scheduled for multiple dates (Groceries, for example might be scheduled for th 1st, 8th, 15th, 22nd, and 29th). In that case, the amounts used for the Calendar calculations have the full amount in the master budget evenly divided among all of the dates. So if the user has defined $1000 for Groceries to be paied on those five dates, each day will have $200 added to 'Payments' for Groceries.
